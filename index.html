Got a shy bladder?

Fu Phobia can help!

What is FU Phobia?

FU Phobia provides virtual practice environments for people with shy bladder. Using a smartphone and VR goggles, FU Phobia users can visit bathrooms all over the world to practice Graduated Exposure Therapy.

How does it work?

There are three quick and easy steps to using FU Phobia:

  1. Join.
  2. Take a quick survey. Rate several bathroom scenarios according to difficulty level for you.
  3. See virtual practice environments organized specifically for you. Start at Level 1 and work your way up, or view the content in any order.

What do I need to use FU Phobia?

Smartphone or iPhone with internet connection

VR goggles for your phone

Earbuds/headphones. You can use FU Phobia without earbuds, but the experience will be much more realistic with them.

How can I try FU Phobia to see if it's right for me?

That's easy! Just check out this virtual practice room. Enter the practice room, put your phone into your VR goggles, put the goggles on and take a test drive. For the most realistic experience possible, wear headphones.
